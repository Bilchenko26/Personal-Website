Bali is a beautiful Island with unseen nature. This is the most popular destination among bloggers. I think there are no people in this world who has never seen at least one picture from that island. Before visiting Bali, I was quite skeptical about all this hustle and hype, but when my foot stepped the land of the jungles, I realized how wrong I was. I’ve never met people who are that sincere, kind and open as local people on Bali. They seem enlightened, they don’t care about material things. It feels like I was on another planet, where people have completely different values in life. I understood why people visit this island to gain a spiritual enlightenment. This island is not only for spiritual practices, but also for having a lot of fun. It has a plenty of clubs, bars and pubs located on the beach, so that people could socialize, drink, listen to music and observe the most beautiful sunsets in their lives. I deeply fell in love with this island, with nature, landscapes, local people and tourists from all around the world. I definitely recommend everyone to visit at list once, but I assure you, you will fall on love with this place as I did.
