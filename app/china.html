Many people who hasn`t been to China imagine this country as extremely crowded and polluted place, where people eat all the insects, they were able to catch. In fact, China is a very developed country with advanced technologies, skyscrapers, yet beautiful parks and comfy restaurants. Even though it seems that China is overpopulated, you won`t see crowds of people pushing each other. I lived in a city with a population over 12 million people and I was always wondering where are those people? Another interesting fact is that it`s almost impossible to meet a Chinese who can speak a decent English. But people there are kind and hospitable, they will use body language to communicate with you. And probably they will ask you to take a picture with them. Chinese food is very unusual to us, Europeans, it might seem disgusting at the first glace, but give it a try and I promise, after some time you will crave it. If you are looking for something unseen before, something unusual and shocking in a pleasant way, I would for sure recommend you visiting China. I cannot wait to come back there as a tourist and explore this lovely country again.
