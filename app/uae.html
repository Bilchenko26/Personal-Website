UAE is a country of “the most”. Everything there is the most expensive, the biggest, the longest. I visited UAE three times and every time I saw something that amazed me. People in this country treat foreigners with respects, no matter what social status or religion they have. Government is trying their best to make life of the citizens comfortable. As you know, weather in UAE can impress with extremely hot temperatures. In summer it can get up to 50 degrees. Nevertheless life in UAE is quite comfortable, as ACs are working everywhere and service got to the point, where you can get everything you need without going out from the car. My brother and his wife live in UAE for almost 8 years and they know how to show Emirats to make people fall in love with this country. I am sure that it doesn’t matter how many times you were there, UAE will always have something new, original and unseen to offer.
